<h1>
Used for the most important heading of the page
<p>
Adding a paragraph of text
<mark>
Used to highlight a piece of text for reference
<sup>
Defines text as being superscript
<cite>
A citation for another source
<em>
A string of emphasized, slightly more important text
<strong>
A string of highly emphasized, much more important text
<blockquote>
A large, stand alone quote from another source
<ul>
Used when the items inside the list do not have a specific order or the order is not important
<ol>
Used when the order of items in the list is important
